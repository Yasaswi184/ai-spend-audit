## Observations

1. Small teams often overspend on enterprise plans.
2. Many users pay for overlapping AI tools.
3. Annual plans can significantly reduce costs.
4. Startups need better visibility into AI SaaS usage.

## Recommendations Logic

- Suggest cheaper plans for small teams.
- Recommend reducing unused seats.
- Identify overlapping subscriptions.s
