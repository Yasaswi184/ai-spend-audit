# User Interviews

## Interview 1

Role:
Frontend Developer

Pain Points:
- Paying for both ChatGPT and Claude
- Unsure which tool provides the most value

Insight:
Users want clear recommendations on overlapping AI subscriptions.

---

## Interview 2

Role:
Startup Founder

Pain Points:
- Team members using separate AI tools
- No centralized tracking of AI spending

Insight:
Teams need visibility into AI SaaS costs.

---

## Interview 3

Role:
Student Developer

Pain Points:
- Limited budget for AI tools
- Confused by multiple pricing plans

Insight:
Affordable plan recommendations are valuable for students and indie builders.

---

## Common Patterns

1. Users frequently overpay for unused plans.
2. Teams often duplicate subscriptions.
3. Many users lack awareness of annual discounts.
4. AI pricing complexity creates decision fatigue.

---

## Product Impact

These interviews influenced:
- simplified audit flow
- savings recommendations
- lightweight MVP approach
