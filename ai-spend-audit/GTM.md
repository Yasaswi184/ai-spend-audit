# Go To Market Strategy

## Target Users

Primary users:
- Startups
- Small AI teams
- Indie hackers
- Developers using multiple AI subscriptions

Secondary users:
- Agencies
- Product teams
- SaaS companies

## Core Problem

Many startups overspend on AI subscriptions because:
- tools overlap
- seats remain unused
- pricing plans are misunderstood

## Acquisition Channels

1. Twitter/X
2. Product Hunt
3. LinkedIn founder communities
4. Reddit startup communities
5. Developer Discord servers

## Launch Strategy

Phase 1:
- Free audit tool
- Collect feedback
- Build waitlist

Phase 2:
- Add advanced analytics
- Introduce team dashboard

Phase 3:
- Subscription model
- Enterprise optimization tools

## Viral Loop

Users share:
- audit reports
- estimated savings
- optimized AI stacks

This creates organic referrals.
